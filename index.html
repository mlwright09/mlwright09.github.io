Online Medical Management System

Pharmatil provides fast and easy communication between medical professionals, patients, and insurance compainies.

  • Stores patient information in their account
  • Pharmacies can store what they have in stock
  • Checks if a prescription is covered by the patient's insurance company
  • Patients can pick their pharmacy with out hassle
  • Informs the pharmacy of new prescriptions
  • Sends a notification to the patient when their prescription is ready for pick up
